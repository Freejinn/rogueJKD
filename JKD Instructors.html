Cheif Instructor Jack O'Shiel

Jack has an extensive background in both martial arts and crisis prevention. He was a counselor for 15 years and currently teaches English courses at University of Colorado, Denver. In time between classes, he trains his wife and both of his children, writes fiction, and travels to where he can fish and surf.
