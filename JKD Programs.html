ADULT PROGRAM

NO LIMITS - NO NONSENSE

Our adult (and mature teen) program meets you at your current fitness level and martial art skill. Our classes range from beginner to expert and provide the opportunity to increase your ability in five areas of competence: technique, tactic, athleticism, emotional content, and situational awareness.

Classes are organized according to 10 student levels and five phases:

PHASE 1: Levels 1-3
Evidence-based self-defense - movement simplicity, knowledge of vulnerable targets, and decision making under pressure.

PHASE 2: Levels 2-5
Martial Sport - increase of fitness and athleticism, effortless transitions between techniques and ranges.

PHASE 3:Levels 5-6
Combat Curriculm - moving beyond self-defense and contact sport, learning to employ weapons, developing deeper levels intensity, and confronting emotional content.

PHASE 4: Levels 7-8
Martial Science - advanced understanding of martial arts and creative application of universal principles.

Phase 5: Levels 9-10
Personal Martial Art - creative expression of your unique self. Refinement of your JKD identity.

YOUTH PROGRAM

CONTROL-FOCUS-FUN

Our youth program is divided into three sections:

  • Cat Kids (ages 5-9)

  • Junior Panthers (ages 9-12)

  • Panthers (ages 12-15)
The youth program takes students from white belt through junior black belt. In the first two stages of learning, our classes emphasize:
  1. strong boudaries against bully behavior
  2. respect for classmates
  3. training partner saftey
  4. mental focus
  5. sustained attention
  6. athletic work-ethic
  7. growth mindset
As students becomes more confident in the basic movements and skills of the martial arts, we teach them to verbally, and if necessary, physically stand against bully aggression and use their self-assurance to take on positive leadership roles in the dojo, in school, and in their community.
